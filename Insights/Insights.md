# Business Insights

This document summarizes the key findings from the data analysis and dashboard.

---

## 1. Booking Completion Rate
Completed rides accounted for **62.06%** of total bookings, indicating a relatively strong fulfillment rate and operational efficiency.

---

## 2. Revenue Contribution
Auto generated the **2623855.00** among all vehicle types, making it the most profitable segment in the dataset.

---

## 3. Payment Preference
Cash was the most preferred payment method as for Booking Status, showing that a significant portion of users still rely on offline payment options.

---

## 4. Customer Rating
The average customer rating was **2.74**, indicating overall customer satisfaction levels across all ride types.

---

## 5. Customer Cancellations
Customer cancellations mainly occurred due to **Change of plans,Wrong Address,Driver is not moving towards pickup location,Driver asked to cancel**.

---

## 6. Driver Cancellations
Driver cancellations were highest due to **Personal & Car related issues,The customer was coughing/sick,Customer related issue,More than permitted people in there**.

---

## 7. Popular Pickup Location
The most popular pickup location was **Ambience Mall**, showing high demand in that area.

---

## 8. Average Booking Value
The average booking value was **344.75**, reflecting the overall spending pattern of customers.

---

## 9. Trip Distance Insights
The longest average trips were generally associated with **Ambience Mall,Shivaji Park,Jasola**, indicating higher-value bookings.

---

## 10. Customer Satisfaction by Vehicle Type
The vehicle type with the highest customer satisfaction was **Auto**, based on average rating scores.

---

## Summary
The analysis highlights key operational patterns such as revenue concentration in specific vehicle types, strong reliance on cash payments, and major cancellation reasons driven by service delays and distance factors. These insights can help improve efficiency, customer experience, and revenue optimization.
