# Uber Ride Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

The Uber Ride Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze ride booking patterns, revenue performance, vehicle utilization, customer behavior, and operational efficiency. The dashboard transforms raw ride data into actionable insights through interactive visualizations, KPIs, and filters.

The project helps stakeholders understand business performance, identify high-performing vehicle categories, monitor booking trends, and evaluate customer preferences.

---

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Overview

Provides a high-level summary of business performance through key metrics and revenue trends.

**Key Metrics:**

* Total Rides
* Total Revenue
* Completed Rides
* Cancellation Rate
* Average Customer Rating
* Average Ride Distance
* Revenue per Ride

---

### 2. Vehicle Performance Analysis

Focuses on vehicle-wise performance and operational insights.

**Analysis Includes:**

* Revenue by Vehicle Type
* Most Booked Vehicles
* Ride Distance by Vehicle
* Vehicle Turnaround Time (VTAT) vs Customer Turnaround Time (CTAT)
* Pickup Location Analysis

---

### 3. Customer & Booking Insights

Examines customer behavior, booking patterns, and payment preferences.

**Analysis Includes:**

* Monthly Booking Trends
* Booking Status Distribution
* Payment Method Preferences
* Vehicle-wise Ratings
* Bookings by Day of Week

---

## Key Business Questions Answered

### Revenue Analysis

* What is the total revenue generated from rides?
* Which vehicle type generates the highest revenue?
* How does revenue vary across months?
* What is the average revenue earned per ride?

### Vehicle Performance

* Which vehicle category receives the most bookings?
* Which vehicle type covers the greatest ride distance?
* How do different vehicle categories compare in operational performance?
* Which vehicles contribute the most to overall business revenue?

### Customer Behavior

* What are the preferred payment methods of customers?
* Which days experience the highest number of bookings?
* How do customer ratings vary across vehicle categories?

### Operational Performance

* What percentage of rides are completed versus incomplete?
* What is the overall cancellation rate?
* Which pickup locations receive the highest number of bookings?
* How efficiently are rides being served across vehicle categories?
